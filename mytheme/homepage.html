Navigating on MagestoreDocs

Search for a Document

Select the Magento version and choose the Extension name to explore its guide.
Or search the Extension by typing its name into the Search bar at the top right of the page

Explore a Document content

When you reach your target extension page, scroll down to view the whole guide.
Or you can use the left navigation bar to go to a particular part of the document

Download a Document

You can download the whole guide of an extension. Simply click on button Download Extension Guide on the top right corner of the extension's page.
